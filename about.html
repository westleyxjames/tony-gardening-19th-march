Our Story

Over 15 years of transforming outdoor spaces with passion and expertise

Founded in 2011, GreenScape Gardening began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional, eco-friendly gardening services that transform outdoor spaces and enhance the beauty of our communities.

What started as a small local operation has grown into one of the most trusted gardening service providers across the United States. We've proudly served over 10,000 satisfied customers, maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Today, our team of 50+ certified gardeners continues to deliver professional services ranging from regular maintenance to complete landscape transformations, always with a focus on environmentally responsible practices.
